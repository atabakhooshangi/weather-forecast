Component Groups:
----------------
[Main Components]
  ├── App.vue (Root component, layout & routing)
  └── HomeView.vue (Main view, orchestrates weather display)

[Weather Components]
  ├── DailyForecast.vue (Daily weather forecasts)
  ├── TemperatureGraph.vue (Temperature trends)
  └── WeatherDisplay.vue (Current conditions)

[Station Components]
  └── StationList.vue (Station management & selection)

Data Flow:
----------
App.vue
  └── HomeView.vue
       ├── DailyForecast.vue (receives forecasts data)
       ├── TemperatureGraph.vue (receives temperature data)
       ├── WeatherDisplay.vue (receives current weather)
       └── StationList.vue (provides station selection)
